# Sample Position — Program Coordinator, Digital Health Initiatives (SYNTHETIC)

Position ID: pos-sample-001
Department: Digital Health Programs
Location: Toronto, ON (hybrid)
Status: Sample fixture for the lab — NOT a real posting.

## Role purpose

Coordinate the planning, stakeholder engagement, and delivery tracking of
digital health interoperability projects across provincial partners.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate project schedules, deliverables, and status reporting across 3–5
  concurrent interoperability projects.
- Prepare briefing materials, meeting minutes, and decision logs for steering
  committees.
- Track risks, issues, and dependencies; escalate per the program framework.
- Support procurement and vendor-coordination activities.
- Liaise with clinical, technical, and policy stakeholders in EN and FR.

## Required qualifications

- 3+ years of project or program coordination experience.
- Experience working with healthcare or public-sector stakeholders.
- Strong written communication: briefings, minutes, status reports.
- Experience with structured risk/issue tracking.

## Preferred qualifications

- PMP, CAPM, or equivalent certification.
- Bilingual EN/FR.
- Familiarity with health-data interoperability standards (e.g. HL7 FHIR).

## Disqualifying criteria

- Unable to work in Canada.
